MARKETING PROCESS

How every project
gets made.

Creative is step four, not step one. The same six steps sit behind every case study on this site.

01

Brief

Define the actual problem before touching the creative — what needs to happen, and how we'll know it worked.

02

Research

Understand the audience before making anything for them — where they spend attention and what they respond to.

03

Strategy

Choose the angle, platforms, formats and message. Strategy is deciding what not to make as much as what to make.

04

Creative

Only now: hooks, scripts, edits, captions — platform-native from the start, because the strategy chose the battlefield.

05

Distribution

Organic, paid, influencer, email, search — each has its own rules. Work is placed where it can actually perform.

06

Performance

Read the results honestly and feed them back into the next brief. This is the step that compounds.
