MARKETING PROCESS

The same six steps,
every project.

Creative work is the fourth step, not the first. This is the order every project on this site follows — tap a step to expand it.

Define the actual problem before touching the creative. What does the brand need to happen — awareness, engagement, conversions — and how will we know it worked?

Understand the audience before making anything for them — who they are, where they spend attention, what they respond to, and what everyone else in the category is already doing.

Decide the angle, the platforms, the formats and the message before production starts. Strategy is choosing what not to make as much as what to make.

Only now: hooks, scripts, shooting, editing, captions and design — built platform-native from the start, because the strategy already decided where this content has to win.

Content is only one part of a marketing system. Organic, paid, influencer, email and search each have their own rules — the work is placed where it can actually perform.

Read the results honestly, learn what worked and why, and feed it back into the next brief. Every case study on this site ends with what I'd improve — because that's the step that compounds.
